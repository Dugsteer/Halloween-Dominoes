HOW TO PLAY HALLOWEEN DOMINOES

Divide into two teams. Click the button to get the first piece and drag it to the centre of the board. Click the button for player 1's first piece. If they can place it, great. Otherwise it must go in a pile on the left. Player 2's pieces go in a pile on the right. Double click the dominoes to turn them. The player who gets "Game Over" wins!
